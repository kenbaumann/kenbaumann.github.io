Ken Baumann

Ken Baumann is a writer, publisher, and book designer. He lives in Los Angeles with his wife, their son, and two dogs.

Ken writes across genres. His latest book is TITLE OF LATEST BOOK, published by PUBLISHER. His previous works include essays, fiction, and poetry published in various journals and anthologies.

As the publisher of Sator Press, Ken has helped bring innovative literary works to readers. He also designs books and has contributed to various literary projects.
